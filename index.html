About me

I am broadly interested in language representations, NLP evaluation and interpretability, uncertainty estimation and probabilistic deep learning. Currently working on uncertainty-aware MT evaluation and aiming to create more trustworthy systems which are robust to the variation observed across different languages and domains.

Before joining IST, I received a MSc and BSc in Computer Science at Higher School of Economics in Moscow, Russia.

When not at my computer, I like to hike and paint with watercolor!
